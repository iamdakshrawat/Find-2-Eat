Annie's Bakery

Annie's Bakery is a charming and inviting bakery located in Clement Town, Dehradun. Our passion for baking is evident in every delicious treat we make, from our perfectly fluffy cakes to our flaky pastries and other baked goods. Our specialty lies in cakes, and we take great pride in crafting cakes that are not only delicious, but also visually stunning. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply treating yourself, you'll find something to love at Annie's Bakery. Our warm and welcoming atmosphere, combined with our friendly staff, makes us the perfect place to stop in for a coffee and a sweet treat. Come visit us today and taste the difference that only Annie's Bakery can offer!
