Jo Paji Paratha Corner

Jo Paji Paratha Corner is a charming and inviting eatery located in Clement Town, Dehradun. Our focus is on serving up delicious, fresh and hot parathas that will satisfy your cravings. Our menu features a wide variety of parathas, priced between INR 30 to 70 each, along with tea, momos, and condiments such as butter and curd, available for just INR 10 each. Each of our parathas is made with care and attention to detail, using the freshest ingredients and traditional spices. Our warm and welcoming atmosphere, combined with our friendly staff, make Jo Paji Paratha Corner the perfect place to enjoy a quick bite or a leisurely meal. Whether you're in the mood for a satisfying breakfast, a delicious lunch, or a light snack, you'll find something to love at Jo Paji Paratha Corner!
