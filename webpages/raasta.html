Raasta Cafe

Raasta Cafe is a modern and trendy cafe located in Clement Town, Dehradun. Our cafe is designed to offer a relaxed and fun atmosphere, with both indoor and outdoor seating options available. Whether you're looking for a cozy corner to catch up with friends or a lively spot to enjoy a meal, Raasta Cafe has got you covered. Our menu features a variety of delicious beverages and light bites, made with the freshest ingredients and contemporary flavors. To add to the ambiance, we play music that sets the perfect mood for a fun and relaxed dining experience. Our knowledgeable and friendly staff is always on hand to assist you and make sure your visit to Raasta Cafe is enjoyable. So come visit us today and experience the vibrant vibe and great food that only Raasta Cafe can offer!
