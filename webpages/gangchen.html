Gangchen Tibet Kitchen

Ganchen Tibet Kitchen is a unique and authentic restaurant located in Clement Town, Dehradun. We offer a delicious blend of Tibetan and Chinese cuisine, each dish made with fresh ingredients and traditional spices. Our menu features a wide range of dishes, from savory stews to stir-fried noodles, and we also offer a selection of refreshing beverages to complement your meal. Our cozy and inviting dining room provides the perfect atmosphere for a casual lunch, a romantic dinner, or a family gathering. Our dedicated staff is passionate about serving you authentic and delicious food, and we strive to make each visit to Ganchen Tibet Kitchen a memorable one. Come experience the flavors of Tibet and China, right here in Dehradun.
